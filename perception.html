VINTAGE BOOK
REDESIGN
“Perception” is a redesign of the book cover of a vintage psychology book that was published in 1968. The purpose of this redesign was to refresh the book cover with a more modern style of imagery. I chose to make the title of the book the main feature of the design and add in representations of eyes, changing the color of the text that is seen through the eyes. This was done in order to represent the shift in perspective that happens when looking at things through other peoples’ eyes. The colors are complimentary orange and blue in order to make them pop off of the page. The final design was then put into a Photoshop mock-up featuring the cover and spine of the book to emulate what the design would look like on an actual book.
