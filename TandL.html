EDITORIAL SPREAD
This Travel and Leisure cover was an imagined cover and editorial spread for the magazine “Travel and Leisure”. I selected Yellowstone National Park as my destination that the cover and featured article would focus on as that is somewhere that I would love to explore someday and I wanted to highlight the beauty of the park and my own appreciation for the outdoors. The project features a cover and two spreads that contain an article about Yellowstone that was found online. All images used in the work are sourced from Unsplash. Creating this spread allowed me to learn how to utilize Adobe InDesign to grasp the technical requirements involved in forming a successful magazine spread.
